# Paid Communities

Paid community landing and gated sections via `@next/monetization-ui`.

## Surfaces

- Community landing with tier cards and benefit preview
- Gated sections — clear locked state, no FOMO countdowns
- Member events panel

## Route

`/community/[slug]` can embed paid community patterns; creator membership at `/[handle]/membership`.

## Philosophy

Belonging over volume — membership gates community participation, not core free content.
