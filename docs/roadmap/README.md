## The roadmap in one paragraph

NEXT evolves through six overlapping phases — core foundation, intelligent
discovery, creator-civilization systems, interactive media, immersive expansion,
and an adaptive semantic ecosystem — toward becoming a discovery engine for human
culture. At 3 years it is a mature human-centered media platform where creators
run media businesses; at 5 years a creator-civilization platform with living
communities and mature AI copilots; at 10 years a semantic media universe where
discovery is cultural-wave navigation and intelligence is woven through the
experience while humans stay culturally central. Every step is bounded by the
invariants already wired into the architecture — resonance over engagement,
fairness over hierarchy, disclosure over deception, coherence over sprawl — and
by [anti-patterns.md](anti-patterns.md), the catalog of failures the whole
roadmap exists to avoid.

## How this roadmap stays honest

- **Grounded** — every horizon is tied to architecture that exists or to a named
  future ADR; no hype-futurism, no forced metaverse, no AI worship.
- **Gated** — speculative phases (especially immersive, Phase E) are gated on
  real-world signals and explicitly permitted to slip indefinitely.
- **Invariant-bounded** — the recommendation, trust, and resilience invariants
  carry forward unchanged into every future phase.
- **ADR-anchored** — the roadmap _names_ the major decisions it will require so
  they are made deliberately, as ADRs, when their phase arrives — not by
  accident.

## Future ADRs the roadmap implies

Collected from the domain docs, to be authored when their phase is scheduled:
an interactive-media content model; a portable creator-identity standard; an
economic-infrastructure/payments architecture; a copilot/AI-governance doctrine;
a multi-surface discovery substrate; a **semantic-discovery substrate redesign**
(the priority infrastructure ADR, ~5-year horizon); an edge-compute platform; a
spatial/volumetric media delivery architecture (Phase-E gated); an
algorithmic-accountability framework; a data-residency/regional-governance
architecture.

## Scope of this phase

Strategy and architecture only. No services, infrastructure, or code were
created or modified. This roadmap is a living document — it is revisited as
horizons approach, as external signals (AI capability, immersive hardware,
regulation) resolve, and as each phase begins.
