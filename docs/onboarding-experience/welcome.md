# Welcome experience

Route: `/welcome` · Package: `@next/onboarding-ui`

## Intent

Cinematic, calm entry — not a signup funnel. Users choose **explore** or **create** without urgency or dark patterns.

## Behavior

- Ecosystem value prop with progressive disclosure
- Path selection persists to `next-onboarding-v1` (local)
- Telemetry: `onboarding_step` with `welcome_path`
